About the Project

Kayumutiara represents a harmonious blend of traditional woodworking techniques and modern design principles. This project showcases the beauty and versatility of wood as a medium for creating functional art.

Each piece is carefully crafted with attention to detail, sustainability, and timeless aesthetics. The name "Mutiara" (meaning "pearl" in Indonesian) reflects the precious nature of these handcrafted wooden creations.

Through this project, we aim to preserve traditional craftsmanship while embracing contemporary design sensibilities, creating pieces that are both beautiful and functional.
